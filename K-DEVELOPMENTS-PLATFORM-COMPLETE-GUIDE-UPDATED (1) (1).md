## 1. WHAT IS THIS PLATFORM? 🎯

**Simple Answer:** A website where people can browse and reserve apartments in your Palencia Al Shorouk project, just like booking a hotel room online.

### **What Problem Does It Solve?**

**Before (Old Way):**

- Customers call you → Wait on phone
- Visit office → Waste time in traffic
- Fill paper forms → Risk of losing documents
- Pay cash → No digital record
- No transparency on which units are available

**After (New Way):**

- Browse all apartments online 24/7
- Reserve instantly with online payment
- All documents digital and organized
- Real-time updates on availability
- Join waiting list if apartment is taken

## 4. THE WAITING LIST SYSTEM 📋

### **How It Works (Simple Explanation):**

**Imagine a bakery:**

- Fresh bread comes out at 3 PM
- People line up before 3 PM
- First person in line gets first bread
- If they don't want it, next person gets it

**Same concept:**

- Unit gets reserved
- Other customers join waiting list
- They're arranged in order (positions 1, 2, 3...)
- If reservation cancelled → Person \#1 gets notified first
- They have 24 hours to reserve
- If they don't → Person \#2 gets notified
- And so on...

***

### **Detailed Flow:**

#### **SCENARIO 1: Someone Joins Waiting List**

**Mohamed wants Unit 304, but it's reserved**

1. Mohamed joins waiting list
2. System checks: 5 people already waiting
3. Mohamed becomes \#6
4. SMS sent: "You're \#6 in line for Unit 304"

**His dashboard shows:**

- Position: \#6 of 6
- People ahead: 5
- Average wait time: 7-10 days

***

#### **SCENARIO 2: Unit Becomes Available**

**Customer cancels reservation for Unit 304**

**What happens automatically:**

**Minute 1:**

- System detects: Unit 304 status changed to "Available"
- Finds waiting list for Unit 304
- Finds person \#1: Sara

**Minute 2:**

- Sara's status changes to "Notified"
- Timer starts: 24 hours countdown

**Minute 3:**

- **SMS sent to Sara:**
"🎉 Great news! Unit 304 is now available! You have 24 hours to reserve it. Click here: [link]"
- **Email sent:**
Full details with photos and reservation button
- **App notification:**
Push notification on phone

**Minute 4:**

- Sara's dashboard shows:
    - Big banner: "YOUR UNIT IS AVAILABLE!"
    - Countdown timer: "23:56 remaining"
    - "Reserve Now" button (glowing)

***

#### **SCENARIO 3A: Sara Reserves Within 24 Hours**

**Hour 5: Sara clicks "Reserve Now"**

1. Goes to checkout page
2. Pays 5,000 EGP
3. Unit reserved to Sara
4. Her waiting list status: "Converted" ✅
5. All others (Mohamed \#2-6) move up positions
6. Mohamed is now \#5

***

#### **SCENARIO 3B: Sara Doesn't Reserve (24 Hours Pass)**

**Hour 25: Timer expires**

**What happens automatically:**

1. Sara's status changes to "Expired"
2. System finds next person: Ahmed (\#2)
3. Ahmed gets notified (same SMS/Email)
4. Ahmed's 24-hour timer starts
5. Sara gets notification: "Opportunity expired"
6. Positions update:
    - Ahmed: \#1 (notified)
    - Mohamed: \#2
    - Fatima: \#3
    - ...

***

## 8. PAYMENT SYSTEM 💳

### **How Payments Work:**

#### **Step 1: Customer Clicks "Pay"**

Customer on checkout page → Clicks "Pay 5,000 EGP"

#### **Step 2: Redirect to Payment Provider**

**Your system sends customer to Paymob/Fawry with:**

- Amount: 5,000 EGP
- Order ID: RES-12345
- Customer info
- Return URL (where to come back after payment)


#### **Step 3: Customer Pays**

**Customer enters:**

- Card number
- Expiry date
- CVV code
- Clicks "Pay"

**Payment provider processes:**

- Checks with bank
- Deducts money
- Sends confirmation


#### **Step 4: Return to Your Website**

**If successful:**

- Customer redirected to: yourwebsite.com/success?order=RES-12345
- Shows success page

**If failed:**

- Customer redirected to: yourwebsite.com/failed
- Shows error message
- "Try again" button


#### **Step 5: Webhook (Behind Scenes)**

**Payment provider sends data to your system:**

```
Transaction successful:
- Order ID: RES-12345
- Amount: 5,000 EGP
- Transaction ID: PAY-789456
- Status: Success
- Time: Jan 16, 2026 3:45 PM
```

**Your system:**

1. Updates reservation status to "Confirmed"
2. Updates payment record
3. Generates receipt PDF
4. Sends confirmation SMS/Email
5. Notifies assigned agent

***

### **Payment Methods Supported:**

**1. Credit/Debit Card:**

- Customer enters card details on Paymob
- Instant confirmation

**2. Fawry:**

- Customer gets reference number
- Goes to any Fawry store
- Tells cashier the number
- Pays cash
- System updates automatically

**3. Vodafone Cash:**

- Customer enters phone number
- Gets SMS with code
- Confirms payment on phone
- Money deducted from mobile wallet

**4. Bank Transfer:**

- Customer manually transfers to your bank
- Uploads transfer receipt
- Agent verifies manually
- Marks as paid in system
